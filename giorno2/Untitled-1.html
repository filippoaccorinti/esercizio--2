Focaccia with caramellized onions,cranberries,and brie.

wow your taste buddy with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion,tart cranberries,and creamy brie,it will become your new favorite holiday starter

Ingredient

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tespoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow oniom
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegear
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries

Method

  1. make the focaccia dough

    2. in a medium-sized mixing bowl , or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knide it a few time...
